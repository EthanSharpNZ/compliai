If someone is seriously hurt, make the area safe and call 111 first. Under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015, a death or other notifiable event must be reported to WorkSafe NZ as soon as possible on 0800 030 040 (deaths: phone immediately, 24/7), the scene must be left undisturbed until an inspector releases it, and the record kept for at least 5 years.
