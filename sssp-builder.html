Plant & equipment register
Plant and equipment on site and its inspection status. EWPs/scissor lifts, scaffold, harnesses and ladders need current inspection records.
Electrical test & tag register
Portable appliances and leads and their test status. AS/NZS 3760 is the NZ standard for in-service testing. Enter dates as dd/mm/yyyy.
Hazardous substances register
Hazardous substances kept or used on site (LPG, fuels, solvents, silica dust), their controls and whether an SDS is held.
Training, licences & competencies
The tickets the job needs. Common NZ examples: EWP/scissor-lift, scaffolding, hot work, confined space, first aid, Site Safe passport, asbestos.
Subcontractors & notifiable works
Subcontractors on site, plus any high-risk work. Some items (scaffold over 5m, excavation over 1.5m, asbestos, demolition, confined space, hot work) need notification to WorkSafe NZ and/or a permit.
